  Tripcount

An android app for managing all your group trips and day to day expenses
1)App contains two modules : day2day expenses and Trip expenses
2) While on Trip, add money paid by any of friend and Tripcount has inbuilt algorithm which automatically calculates which friend owes money to whom and how they can settle the payments.
3)It also keeps track of day to day expenses, you can add expenses for a particular friend and it will show total amount owed by that friend.
