  SnapLook

A photo gallery app
1) Application developed in Django(python)
2) It extracts images of particular hash tag from twitter and display the images in form of image gallery
3) Get all the images you looked for in past, use #msdeep14
4) It also analyze the facial expression(anger, contempt, disgust, fear, happiness, neutral, sadness and surprise) of image using MICROSOFT emotion API.
